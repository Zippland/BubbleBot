Call this ONLY during a heartbeat scan of a group chat when you decide NOT to chime in.

The heartbeat scan periodically wakes you to look at recent group messages that didn't @ you. If after reading them you judge the bot should not speak right now (off-topic, already answered by others, casual chatter you have nothing to add to, etc.), call this tool. After calling, the agent loop ends and nothing is sent to the group.

If you DO think the bot should speak, just reply normally — do NOT call this tool. This tool is only available during heartbeat turns; if you don't see a "[heartbeat scan]" preamble in the user message, this tool will not be available.
